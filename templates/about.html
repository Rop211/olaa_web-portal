✝ Our Story

Building Strong Foundations
in Faith and Knowledge

Our Lady of Assumption Academy (OLAA) is a premier Catholic primary school in ELDORET SOY dedicated to nurturing young minds and building strong foundations for lifelong success.

Founded in 1995, OLAA has grown from a small kindergarten to a full primary school serving over 450 students. Our commitment to excellence in education, faith formation, character development, and holistic growth sets us apart.

As a Catholic school, we are guided by the teachings of the Catholic Church and the vision of Our Lady of Assumption. We strive to form students who are intellectually competent, morally upright, and spiritually grounded.

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic Catholic education that nurtures the whole person – intellectually, spiritually, and morally – preparing children for a life of service to God and community.

✝ Catholic Identity

Rooted in Faith

As a Catholic school, OLAA is committed to the integral formation of the human person. We believe that education is not just about academic excellence but about forming the whole person in the image of Christ.

Our students participate in daily prayers, weekly Mass, and religious education classes. We celebrate the liturgical seasons and foster a deep love for the Eucharist and the Blessed Virgin Mary.

Daily Prayer

Morning prayers, grace before meals, and evening prayers

Weekly Mass

Students attend Mass every Friday and on Holy Days of Obligation
